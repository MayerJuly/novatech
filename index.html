PETBOT

Imagine a world where single-use plastic bottles bound for landfills could be recycled into new, useful products right at home. With the PETBOT, all of this is possible! PET plastic is a durable, safe and is comparable to PETG filament, which is commonly used in 3D printing. With PET filament, made with the PETBOT, you can print custom parts for machines, toys, decorations, and more! Many parts of the PETBOT are printed with our PET filament. Everything from our bearing cutter guide to our motor gears, which experience heavy forces while pulling the plastic ribbon through the extrusion nozzle. This machine is great for all ages. Get your kids and teens involved in recycling, and inspire others to be recyclers. The PETBOT takes any PET bottle, no matter the size or color; making it ideal for plastic recycling beginners!
